Objective and agenda

-We serve to reinforce drug education, as well as correct public opinion relating to substance abuse.
-Document essential aspects of common substances.
-Explain, with the sake of brevity in mind, the pharmacology of substances.
-Advocate for further harm reduction practices

Precautions

Safety measures differ from substance to substance, however, there is a consensus as to certain procedures that are universally accepted.
-Measure your dose
-Test your substance
For further information, https://psychonautwiki.org/wiki/Responsible_drug_use
